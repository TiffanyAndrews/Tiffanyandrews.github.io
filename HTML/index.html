    About Me

    Tiffany Andrews is a budding full-stack developer working with HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, and Ruby on Rails. As Tiffany pursued a career in government administration and urban planning, her love for technology remained throughout. She began to build her technological foundation through data visualization using Tableau, UX data analysis and organizing an annual STEM competition for Long Beach youth. As an employee with the City of Long Beach, her talent & value were recognized as she was put on an accelerated track to achieving the role of Chief of Staff within 3 years. She worked on streamlining public interaction with government through the implementation and promotion of technological, citizen-focused applications in addition to managing over 20 community events, programs and projects. As a citizen of the world, she is driven by the evolvement of community and technology with a solution-centric passion as seen in her volunteer work with Junior League of Long Beach. In that role she provides structure, vision and training on the Strategic Planning committee and serves as Chair of Training. She is a contributor to the open-source projects with Code for America Long Beach Brigade and creates, supports as well as promotes female engineers as a Co-Organizer for the Women Who Code Los Angeles chapter.
